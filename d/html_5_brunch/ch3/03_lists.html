Recipe of the Day:Honeydew Melon

  • 3 cups Honeydew Melon (seeded & chopped)
  • 2 tsp Lime Juice
  • 1 cup Vanilla Nonfat Yogurt
  • 1 cup Ice Cubes
  1. Take a blender, add honeydew melon and watermelon; blend until it smoothens.
  2. Sample your smoothie and add honey or sugar if needed. Blend again.
  3. Mix yogurt, ice and limejuice and blend it again.
  4. Transfer it into tall glasses and drink it immediately.
